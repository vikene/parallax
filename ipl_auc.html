Be The Manager



The Auction

1) The auction shall take place on two days(26/3 and 27/3) for the preliminary rounds and on 2/4 for the final round.
2) A total of 150 players will be under hammer.(40 bat+17 wk+31 all+62 bowl)
3) The auctions shall take place like the ipl auctions.
4) The decision of the auctioneer is final.
5) Initial amount given is rs.60 crore.
6) Auctions will be done in indian rupees only.
7) Team can contain min of 14 players and max of 20 players.
Note: not more than 3 players from the same intl team can be bought.
8) THE TEAMS NEED TO SUBMIT THEIR PLAYING XI EVERYDAY AND THEY GET POINTS WITH THE PLAYERS PERFORMANCE IN THE Wt20 matches on that day.
9) From the prelims three teams(table toppers only) will advance to the final round.
10) The final round shall have three teams participating and 60 players will be under the hammer.
11) The team with the highest points will be declared winner.
12) Each playing Xi should contain minimum of a keeper, an all-rounder, 4 batsmen & 3 bowlers.

Points System (batsman)

1) 1 point for every run scored
2) 2 points for a boundary, 4 points for a sixer
3) 4 points per 25 runs
4) -5 for duck out
5) -3 for hit wicket, obs the field, hand. The ball



Points System (bowlers)


1) 10 points per wkt.
2) 5 points per maiden over
3) 1 point per dot ball
4) -2 for boundary, -4 for sixer
5) -1 for no-balls and wides

Points System(fielders)


1) 10 points for indirect run outs and catches.
2) 15 for direct run outs.
3) 5 for stumping(keepers)



Bonuses


1) points achieved in 20th over will be doubled.
2) If your player creates a new record he gets 30 points.
3) Ending up on the winning side gives 25 points.
4) the captain gets double the points.
5) so don't forget to mention your captain.
