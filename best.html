Best out of waste

Event Description:

Best out of waste is a challenging event in anubhava 14 providing a platform for the students for their creativity. the event is based on using various waste materials in daily life for creating structures or designs.



Rules and Regulations:

1. Maximum of 2 members per team
2. Students should submit their abstract on or before 27th march 2014
3. Materials are provided by the organizing committee based on the abstract submitted.
4. In case of discrepancies , organizers decision is final
5. the overall usage of money for the project should not exceed rs100
6. Students should mail their abstracts to bestoutofwaste@anubhava14.in
