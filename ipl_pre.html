Game On!



Event Details:

Game On! has four different pc games.
1. Age of Empires - 2 players per team
2. DOTA - 5 players per team
3. Counter Strike - 5 players per team
4. Need for speed-Most Wanted - single player

Fee: 100/- per team for DOTA,CS,AOE
30/- per player for NFS



Rules:

1. Each player can participate only once.
2. Each team should register only once and should give the complete details of the team during registration.
3. Game rules like Map, Population, Game time, etc., will be on spot decision by the organizer.
4. Game play is based on number of teams registered. Either a league or knockout tournament.
5. Teams are requested to get their own laptops to play, with good LAN connectivity.
6. Any team not following the rules will be considered as eliminated.
7. Organizer decision will be final during any clash or tie.
